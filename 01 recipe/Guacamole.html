Pomegranate Guacamole

Ingredients

  • 4 ripe avocados (2 pounds total)
  • 1 cup finely chopped white onion
  • 2 fresh serrano chiles, finely chopped including seeds
  • 1/4 cup fresh lime juice
  • 3/4 cup pomegranate seeds
  • 3/4 cup diced peeled mango
  • 1/2 cup chopped cilantro

Preperation

  1. Halve, pit, and peel avocados. Coarsley mash in bowl. Stir in onion, chiles, 1/4 cup lime juice, and 1 1/4 teaspoons salt.
  2. Then fold in pomegranate seeds, mango, and cilantro.
  3. Let guacamole be chilled for 4 hours prior to consumption
